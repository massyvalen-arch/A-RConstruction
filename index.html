Our Story

Built on craft,
measured in years.

We don't pour concrete. We build the grounds of a home — the first impression, the last detail, the surface your family walks on for a generation.

A&R Construction Group is a family-run masonry and landscape construction firm serving the Greater Toronto Area. From classical stone entrances to layered backyard retreats, every project is handled by our own crews — no subcontracting the finish, no cutting corners on the base.

Our work lives outside. It has to survive Canadian winters, spring thaws, and twenty years of footsteps. So we build it to.

What we do
— and what we do best.

Six disciplines, one standard. Every surface we lay, wall we raise, and garden we shape is measured against the same uncompromising benchmark.

Paver Driveways
